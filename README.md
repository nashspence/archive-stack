# Archive Storage MVP

A small, heavily opinionated self-hosted archive service for households, home labs, and small groups that want to move files out of instant-access storage and onto offline optical discs without losing track of them. It catalogs uploads, automatically packs them into configured disc-sized archive sets, and prepares them for ISO creation and offline storage. Files that have not yet been burned, along with files explicitly kept cached, stay available on the filesystem under their original uploaded paths, while archived files remain visible in the catalog even when their data is offline. It is built for this specific workflow, not as a general-purpose storage platform or polished backup product.
